The coolest creature

The Cheetah is awesome because Cheetahs are the fastest land animals, capable of reaching speeds of up to 120 km/h. Their flexible spine allows them to cover an impressive 7 meters in a single stride. With non-retractable claws for better grip during sprints, they are perfectly built for speed. Tear-like facial markings help reduce glare and improve focus on prey, and their unique spot patterns make each cheetah as distinctive as a fingerprint. .
