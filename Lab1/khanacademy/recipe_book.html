Recipe #1 Chicken Stir-Fry

  • Prep Time: 10 minutes
  • Cook Time: 15 minutes
  • Serves: 2-3
Ingredients Quantity
chicken breasts (sliced thinly)2
bell pepper 1
carrot 1
soy sauce2 tbsp
oyster sauce1 tbsp
sesame oil1 tbsp
cornstarch (optional)1 tsp
sesame seeds(optional)1

Instructions:
Step 1: Heat sesame oil in a pan and cook the chicken until golden. Remove and set aside.
Step 2: Stir-fry the vegetables until tender-crisp.
Step 3: Add soy sauce, oyster sauce, and cornstarch mixed with a little water to thicken.
Step 4: Toss the chicken back in and stir to combine. Sprinkle with sesame seeds before serving.

Recipe #2 Classic Caesar Salad

  • Prep Time: 10 minutes
  • Cook Time: None (if using pre-cooked chicken)
  • Serves: 2
IngredientsQuantity
Romaine lettuce 1/2 cup
Croutons1/2 cup
Parmesan cheese 2 tbsp
Caesar dressing2 tbsp
Chicken breast (optional, grilled or sliced) 1

Instructions:
Step 1: Wash and chop the lettuce, then toss with Caesar dressing.
Step 2: Add croutons and Parmesan cheese.
Step 3: Top with sliced grilled chicken for a heartier option.

Recipe #3 Homemade Pizza

  • Prep Time: 15 minutes (if using pre-made dough)
  • Cook Time: 10-15 minutes
  • Serves: 2-3

Ingredients:

  • Pizza dough (store-bought or homemade)
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Toppings of choice (pepperoni, mushrooms, olives, bell peppers, etc.)

Instructions:
Step 1: Preheat the oven to 220°C (425°F).
Step 2: Roll out the pizza dough and spread with tomato sauce.
Step 3: Add shredded mozzarella and your favorite toppings.
Step 4: Bake for 10-15 minutes or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbling.
